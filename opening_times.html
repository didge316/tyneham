Tyneham Opening Times 2026

Lulworth Range walks and the historic village of Tyneham are normally open to visitors on most weekends throughout the year, offering the chance to explore both Tyneham village and its unique stretch of coastline.

Visitors are warmly welcomed to enjoy the walking routes, scenery and heritage of this special area. Please note, however, that public access will not be available on the following dates in 2026 due to scheduled closures:

Other Special Dates Through 2026 When Tyneham Village Will Be Open

Christmas & New Year - Open daily from Saturday 20 December 2025 right through to Sunday 4 January 2026. A perfect time to enjoy the peaceful winter countryside and festive atmosphere around Tyneham and the Lulworth Range walks.

Easter - Open every day from Saturday 28 March to Sunday 12 April 2026 (Easter Sunday falls on 5 April). Great for spring walks and exploring the village when everything is coming back to life.

Early May Bank Holiday - Open daily from Saturday 2 May to Monday 4 May 2026 - a lovely long weekend to make the most of the Dorset coast and countryside.

Spring Stand-Down - Open every day from Saturday 23 May to Sunday 31 May 2026, giving you a full week to wander the range walks and visit Tyneham without interruption.

Summer Stand-Down - Open daily throughout the main summer period, from Saturday 25 July to Sunday 31 August 2026. Ideal for longer days out, picnics, and exploring Worbarrow Bay and the surrounding hills.

Opening and Closing Times

After weekday firing finishes, the gates to the range walks usually open on Friday afternoons - they aim for around 4:30pm, but the exact time depends on range clearance and the weather. Once open, you can enjoy the walks right through the weekend until the gates close again at 8am on Monday morning.

The vehicle gate to Tyneham village itself opens at 9am on days when there's no military activity, and closes again at dusk each evening.

If you'd like to explore the history in more depth, the exhibitions in Tyneham School and Church are open from 10am to 4pm on non-firing days - well worth a visit for a real feel of what life here was like.
