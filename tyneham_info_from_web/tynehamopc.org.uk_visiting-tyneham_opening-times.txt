On what days is Tyneham open to the public?
Tyneham and the Lulworth Range Walks are open
most weekends
and
all main public holidays
.
They are open to the public each weekend except for the following weekends
:
17 and 18 January 2026
= CLOSED
14 and 15 March 2026
= CLOSED
13 and 14 June 2026 = CLOSED
12 and 13 September 2026 = CLOSED
10 and 11 October 2026 = CLOSED
14 and 15 November 2026 = CLOSED
In addition to the weekends,
they are open every day during the following holiday periods
–
all dates are inclusive:
Christmas Break: 20 December 2025 to 4 January 2026 = OPEN
Easter Break: 28 March to 12 April 2026 = OPEN
Early May Bank Holiday: 2 May to 4 May 2026 = OPEN
Late Spring Break: 23 May to 31 May 2026 = OPEN
Summer Break: 25 July to 31 August 2026 = OPEN
Christmas Break: 19 December 2026 to 3 January 2027 = OPEN
At what times is Tyneham open?
On the days when Tyneham is open:
The
Elmes Grove gate
that allows
vehicle access
to Tyneham is
opened at 9am
each day
and is
locked each evening at dusk
.
The
exhibitions in
Tyneham Church
,
Tyneham Farm
and
Tyneham School
are
opened at 10am
and
closed at 4pm
.
The
gates to the walks
are
opened
around 4.30pm on Friday
and
closed at 8am on Monday
(when only open at weekends).
Important
The opening dates and firing times are subject to last-minute change. Please phone 01929 404714 to listen to an answering machine that will give up-to-date access information to callers.
For general information phone 01929 404714.
