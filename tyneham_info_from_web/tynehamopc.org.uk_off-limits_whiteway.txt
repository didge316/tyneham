Whiteway
Off Limits:
Whiteway Farm and the whole of Whiteway are strictly off limits at all times.
Properties immediately north and north-east of Whiteway Farm
Whiteway
, sometimes referred to as West Whiteway, lies to the north of
Tyneham
and west of
Povington
. Sadly the whole area is off limits and its former residents are often forgotten.
