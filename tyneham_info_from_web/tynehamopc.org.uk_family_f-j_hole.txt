After
Tyneham School
closed in 1932, sisters
Charlotte Hole (1869-1948)
, known as
Lottie
,  and
Helen Hole (1874-1958)
lived at
1 The Row – The School House
. The cottage was given the name
Flagstaff Cottage
.
Photo courtesy of Lindsey Guest
On Sunday 11 June
1939
Helen
fell down a cliff
near
Worbarrow
and
suffered multiple head injuries
. She was taken to
Swanage Cottage Hospital
. According to the newspaper report shown below, the hospital described her condition as ‘very comfortable’. Over three months later, on 29 September 1939, Helen was still in hospital.
Western Gazette, 16 June 1939
Meanwhile, sister
Lottie
was living in Corfe View Road, Corfe Mullen next door to brother
William Hole (1871-1958)
and his wife. With Lottie
were
the
Cossars
, with whom sister Helen had once lived.
