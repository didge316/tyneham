2003: Death of John Durant-Lewis
By
admin
9 April 2003
26 August 2024
Obituary
DURANT-LEWIS JOHN (Late Chief Executive of Wareham & Purbeck Rural
District Council) and resident of Wareham for nearly 70 years, passed away
peacefully, aged 93 years, at Holmegate Residential Home, on 5th April 2003.
Funeral Service at Poole Crematorium on Tuesday 15th April at 1.30pm. Family
flowers only please. All enquiries c/o Lesley Shand Funeral Service, Tel:
(01202) 658833.
Published by the Bournemouth Echo, 9 April 2003
