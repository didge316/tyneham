1920: Tyneham – War Memorial
By
admin
25 November 1920
26 August 2024
Newspaper
,
War Dead
By the
unveiling and dedication
of the
memorial tablet
to the members of the parish who made the
supreme sacrifice
in the
Great War
the parish of Tyneham has worthily
perpetuated the memory
of its
gallant sons
, and also contributed its quota to the war memorials springing up all over the country.
The tablet is of
white marble
on a foundation of
black marble
.
The unveiling and dedication took place at a special service held on Friday evening. The
Rural Dean (Canon Blackett) performed the ceremony
and gave an inspiring address on the “Unknown Soldier,”, taking as his text II. Cor. vi., 9.
Published by the Dorset County Chronicle, Thursday 25 November 1920
