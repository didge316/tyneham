1935: Death of John Ephraim Moore
By
admin
27 May 1935
26 August 2024
Obituary
The death occurred at Worbarrow Bay, Tyneham, on Saturday, of John Ephraim Moore, the two-years-old son of Mr. and Mrs. Ephram Moore, of New Street, Bulkington.
The boy, who had only recently recovered from a long illness, had been taken to Worbarrow Bay for a short holiday, and it was after being taken ill again that he died on Saturday.
Published by the Coventry Evening Telegraph, 27 May 1935
