House 2 Family
Herbert John House (1889-1958)
Herbert was born at
East Chaldon
on 9 February 1889. He was the son of
William House (1848-1934)
, born at East Knighton, and his wife
Mary Jane House nee Hammett (1848-1908)
, born at Tolpuddle. Mary’s mother was a
Loveless
and those familiar with the
Tolpuddle Martyrs
will recognise the surnames of
Hammett
and
Loveless
.
Herbert was baptised at Chaldon Herring on 17 March 1889. His father William’s trade was recorded as carpenter.
Two years later, Herbert was living in the Dairyhouse at
Affpuddle
with his parents and four older siblings. His father William was now a dairy farmer. By 1901 his father was farming at Fossil Farm, East Chaldon which he continued until his death in 1934.
We have not yet been able to establish where Herbert was living at the time of the 1901 census.
In 1911, Herbert was living with his mother and father at Fossil Farm.
Herbert married
Jessie Emily Pitcher Bowditch (1893-1978)
–
By 1915
