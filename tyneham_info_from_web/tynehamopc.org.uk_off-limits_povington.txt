Off Limits:
Povington is strictly off limits at all times.
Povington
lies to the north of
Tyneham
and east of
Whiteway
. It was home to many families over the generations. Sadly the whole area is off limits and its former residents are often forgotten. It is now home to an automated railway-based moving target system for firing practice.
Below we show the likely location of the former properties of Povington based on information from various sources and personal accounts.
