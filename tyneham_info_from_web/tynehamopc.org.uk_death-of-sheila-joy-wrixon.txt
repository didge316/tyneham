2017: Death of Sheila Joy Wrixon
By
admin
4 January 2018
31 August 2023
Obituary
Sheila WRIXON Joy (Of Barwick Near Yeovil) In her 90th year, peacefully on Christmas day at her home surrounded by her family. Much loved by all her family and friends. Funeral service will take place at Yeovil Crematorium on Wednesday 10th January 2018 at 2pm. Family flowers only please. Donations in memory of Sheila for either St. Margaret’s Hospice, Yeovil or Yeovil Heartbeat may be sent c/o Clive Wakely A.J. Wakely & Sons 33 Sparrow Road Yeovil BA21 4BT Tel : 01935 479913 Please make cheques payable to the charity.
