Tyneham House
The ruins of Tyneham House are NOT accessible to members of the public.
The steps from Tyneham House were removed to Bingham’s Melcombe, a private house and estate not open to the public.
The north porch doorway from Tyneham House now stands proudly at the head of ‘The Canal’ at Athelhampton House (see below).
