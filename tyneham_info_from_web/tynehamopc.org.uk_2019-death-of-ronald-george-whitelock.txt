By

Obituary
WHITELOCK Ronald George (Ron) Passed away peacefully in Poole Hospital on 11th March 2019, aged 83 years. Loving and dearest husband to Myrtle, much loved father to Lionel, daughter-in-law Louise, a dear brother-in-law, uncle & friend. Happy memories of Morden Football Club Funeral Service at Poole Crematorium on Wednesday 27th March 2019 at 2:30pm. Family flowers only please. Donations if desired for Dorset & Somerset Air Ambulance can be made online at www.funeraldirector.co.uk/ron-whitelock or sent c/o Ives & Shand Funeral Service 568 Ashley Road, Parkstone, Poole, Dorset BH14 0AG Tel: 01202 716500
