Stickland family
have lived in the parish of
Tyneham
, spanning the period from
1804 or before
through to the forced evacuation in
1943
.
The Sticklands had been fishermen and boatbuilders for generations and bore the reputation of being “able to do anything with their hands”.
Lilian Bond
