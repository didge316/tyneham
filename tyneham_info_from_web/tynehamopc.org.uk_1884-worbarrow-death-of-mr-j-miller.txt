1884: Worbarrow – Death of Mr. Joseph Miller
By
admin
18 April 1884
26 August 2024
Obituary
It is with regret that we announce the death of Mr. Joseph Miller, an old and respected inhabitant of this place.
He was a great favourite with both old and young, and will be greatly missed. In fact Worbarrow has lost its chairman.
He gently fell asleep on March 30th last at the good old age of 83 years. He was interred in the quiet little churchyard at Tyneham on the Friday following his death, and the greatest respect was shown by all the friends and neighbours around him.
Published by Blandford and Wimborne Telegram, Friday 18 April 1884
