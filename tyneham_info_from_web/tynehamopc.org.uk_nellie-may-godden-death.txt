By

Obituary
GODDEN NELLIE MAY Peacefully on 5th October at White Lodge Rest Home, aged 105 years. Sadly missed by all her family and friends. Service Rossmore Gospel Church, Rossmore Road, Parkstone on Thursday 22nd October at 1.45 p.m., followed by interment at Wareham Cemetery. Family flowers only please, but, if desired, donations made payable to the ‘Tear Fund’ may be sent to George Scott Funeral Services, 15 Somerset Road, Boscombe, BH7 6JH. Tel: 01202 395827
Bournemouth Daily Echo 19 October 2009
