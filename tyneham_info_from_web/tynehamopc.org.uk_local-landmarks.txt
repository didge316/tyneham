Flower’s Barrow
Flowers Barrow
is an
iron-age hillfort
on the cliff edge above
Worbarrow Bay
. Sadly around half of the original hillfort has been lost due to continual erosion of the cliffs.
Flowers Barrow is only accessible when
Tyneham
and the
Lulworth Range
walks are open – see
Opening Dates & Times
. It can be reached either by a steep coastal path from Worbarrow or by a path from Whiteway Hill.
