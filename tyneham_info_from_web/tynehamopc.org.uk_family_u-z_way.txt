The
Way Family
lived at
Tyneham Farm
Dairy
from
1890
to no later than 1901 and at
North Egliston Farm
, Tyneham from
1901 or before
until
1906
Frederick Thomas Way (1864-1943)
Wedding of Frederick’s sister Melina Way to Walter Voss on 4 December 1900
Frederick was born at
Toller Porcorum
, Dorset on 21 February 1864. He was the son of farmer
Thomas Way (1840-1904)
and his wife
Anne Hayward Way nee Walbridge (1837-1906)
.
In 1881 the family were at
Nether Kingcombe Dairy House
and Frederick, aged 17, was shown as a
Dairyman’s son
. By April 1890 Frederick had moved to
Tyneham
.
Frederick married
Bessie Elizabeth Mary Howard (1866-1954)
at
Frampton
, Dorset on 8 April 1890. Frederick’s abode was given as
Tyneham
and Bessie’s as
Southover
. a hamlet near Tolpuddle, Dorset.
Bessie was born on 11 March 1866 at
Owermoigne
, Dorset. Her parents were Suffolk-born
William Howard (1821-1906)
, a dairyman, and his second wife
Maria Howard nee Suttle (1825-1905)
. Bessie was the sister of
Charles William Howard (1860-1928)
.
In 1891 Frederick aged 26 and Bessie aged 25 were living at
Tyneham Farm Dairy
, along with Frederick’s sister
Melissa Olga Way
aged 22 and a dairy servant
Albert Wort
from Redlynch aged 19.
Children of Frederick and Bessie
Frederick and Bessie had eight children, one of whom died shortly after birth:
Thomas Way (1891-?)
– Thomas was born on 6 May 1891 at Tyneham and baptised there on 31 May 1891 – Thomas married
Gertrude Mabel Cornick (1894-1968)
at
Folke
, Dorset on 23 Apr 1918 – Thomas’ brother James was one of the witnesses – Thomas was living at
Gussage All Saints
, Dorset – in 1939 Thomas and Gertrude were at 5 Burcombe Lane, Wilton with baby daughter
Jeannie
James Frederick Way (1893-1975)
– James was born at Tyneham on 7 January 1893 and baptised there on 12 March 1893 –  – did not marry – Wyke Farm, Wimborne St Giles
Reginald William Way (1895-1968)
– Reginald served as a
Gunner
(152300) with the
102nd Siege Battery, Royal Garrison Artillery
–  Reginald married
Adelaide Attwood
on 26 November 1928 at Wimborne St. Giles, Dorset – Westwood Farm, Corfe Castle – Reginald died 13 November 1968
Westwood Farm was home to Reginald
Gertrude Mabel Way (1896-1978)
– did not marry – Wyke Farm, Wimborne St Giles
Cecil Arthur Edgar Way (1898-1982)
– – did not marry – Wyke Farm, Wimborne St Giles
Hilda Dorothy Way (1900-1986)
– married Josiah Humphrey Coombes (1895-1958)
Edgar Leslie Way (1902-1902)
– died aged just 10 days
Gladys Ena Way (1906-2003)
– Gladys was born on 12 October 1906 at Steeple and baptised on 26 November 1906 at Tyneham – in 1911 she was – did not marry – Wyke Farm, Wimborne St Giles
Wyke Farm was home to James Frederick Way and three siblings: Gertrude, Cecil & Alice
