Tyneham Action Group
The Tyneham Action Group was formed on 18 May 1968 in a meeting room at the Moule Institute, Fordington, Dorchester. It followed a plea in the first issue of ‘Dorset – The County Magazine’ to form the group.
A committee was formed with engineer Philip Draper, formerly of Sheepleaze, Worbarrow as its Chairman and journalist and campaigner Rodney Legg as its Secretary.
