Newspaper
Mr. William Ralph Garneys Bond
, of
Tyneham House
, Corfe Castle,
Dorset’s new sheriff
, was born in 1880, was educated at Eton and New College, Oxford. He served in the Sudan political service from 1905 to 1926 and was governor of Fung (1922-24) and of Dongola (1924-26). He is a Justice of the Peace for Dorset, having qualified at the 1927 Midsummer Quarter Sessions, and is a member of the Wareham and Purbeck Rural Council.
Published in Western Gazette, 6 April 1945
