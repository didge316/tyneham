The
Wellman Family
lived at
Gwyle Cottages
from about 1912 until 1936.
Robert Wellman (1887-1964)
Bob was born at Upwey in Dorset to
Walter Wellman
and his wife
Susan Wellman nee Cox
.
Bob married
Alice Victoria Lucas (1887-1975)
, the daughter of ‘Shepherd Lucas’ and his wife Jane who lived in Post Office Row – see the
Lucas
family page.
Alice and Bob c.1950
Bob and Alice had six children:
Alice Edith Rose Wellman (1912-1992)
–
Rose
worked as a servant for the Bond Family at
Tyneham House
. In 1934 she married
Joseph Alfred Dando (1912-2002)
. Joe was also a servant at
Tyneham House
, having worked there from 1926. Rose and Joe had twelve children – seven boys and five girls.
Vera Winifred Wellman (1918-1970)
–
Vera
married
Herbert Arthur Stockley (1917-1995)
in 1941 and they had one son
Ivan Owen Stockley (1944-1987)
.
Dennis James Wellman (1919-2004)
–
Jim
married in 1950 and had a son and a daughter.
Ivy Jane Wellman (1921-1997)
–
Ivy
married in 1946 and had a daughter and a son.
Peter Wellman
Freda Joyce Wellman (1927-2010)
–
Joyce
married and had three sons.
The family left Tyneham approximately seven years before the evacuation and in September 1939 were living at East Whiteway Farm Cottages near Church Knowle.
In October 1979, the three surviving Wellman sisters, Rose, Ivy and Joyce, returned to Tyneham for the first church service to be held there in 36 years.
