1910: Parish Meeting
By
admin
25 March 1910
25 August 2024
Newspaper
The
annual parish meeting
was held in the
Schoolroom
on Saturday evening, the Rector (the
Rev. C. S. Homan
) being in the chair.
Mr. W. H. Bond
1
proposed the re-election of Mr. Homan
2
as chairman.
Mr J. Bascombe
3
seconded, and it was carried unanimously. Messrs.
Job Chilcott
and
Walter C. Smith
4
were unanimously re-elected
overseers
, and thanks were accorded them for their past services.
The Chairman announced that Mr. W. H. Bond had kindly became guarantee to the
Postmaster-General
for the necessary amount required for the restoration of the
postal telegraph service
to Tyneham, which was suspended on the removal of the
coastguard
, and a hearty vote of thanks to Mr. Bond for his action was carried with acclamation. The restoration of the telegraph service will prove a great boon to Tyneham.
The Chairman was cordially thanked for presiding.
Published by Dorset County Chronicle, Thursday 24 March 1910
.
