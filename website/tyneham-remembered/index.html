The Real Story of Tyneham: Told by the Villagers Themselves

Rare interviews with the last residents of Dorset's "forgotten village" - captured before they passed away. A moving and unique piece of living history.

Watch the opening of the documentary, featuring the last residents of Tyneham speaking in their own words.

What's in the documentary?

Tyneham Remembered brings together the personal accounts of people who were born and grew up in the village, filmed before the last survivors passed away. In their own words, they describe daily life in Tyneham, the shock of the 1943 evacuation order, and what it felt like to return to the ruins of their childhood homes. To understand the full story, read the complete history of Tyneham or explore what makes Tyneham Dorset's most famous ghost village.

  • ✓  Interviews with former residents Arthur Grant, Peter Wellman and Doug Churchill
  • ✓  Personal accounts of everyday life in the village before the evacuation
  • ✓  First-hand descriptions of receiving the order to leave, just days before Christmas 1943
  • ✓  Emotional return visits to the ruins of their homes
  • ✓  Rare archive photographs of the village in its final years
