## AI Search Readiness — 62%

**PASS:**
- `llms.txt` live and accessible (HTTP 200) ✅
- Well-structured: site description, key page list with absolute URLs, key facts ✅
- FAQPage schema on homepage aids AI citation ✅
- Named author with bio page — entity signals for AI systems ✅
- TouristAttraction + geographic coordinates — machine-readable location data ✅

**ISSUES:**
- **Cloudflare managed robots.txt blocks major AI crawlers** — GPTBot (ChatGPT), ClaudeBot (Claude), Google-Extended (Gemini training), CCBot, Bytespider are all `Disallow: /`. This is Cloudflare's default AI protection feature (`ai-train=no`). Blocking training bots is reasonable; but blocking GPTBot also prevents ChatGPT's retrieval-augmented responses from citing the site. Review in Cloudflare Dashboard → Security → Bots → AI Scrapers.
- **Common Crawl**: Zero referring domains in sample — domain authority very low (PageRank 4.5e-9). AI systems weight authoritative sources. Link building needed for long-term AI citability.
