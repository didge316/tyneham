Tyneham Village Map

Tyneham Village is located on the beautiful Dorset coast, roughly between Lulworth and Corfe Castle. The nearest populated towns include Kimmeridge and West Lulworth (not shown on this map).

All of the surrounding area and towns are truly wonderful with plenty of accommodation including guest houses, hotels, campsites and bed & breakfast.

Swanage, Corfe Castle, Wareham and Lulworth are all very nice places to stay, to name but a few.
