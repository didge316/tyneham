Can You Camp at Tyneham?

Tyneham’s peaceful setting on the Jurassic Coast makes it a tempting spot for an overnight stay, but camping inside the village itself isn’t allowed – it’s a preserved historic site within the Lulworth military range.

The good news is that there are plenty of excellent campsites within easy reach, giving you a comfortable base for exploring Tyneham, Worbarrow Bay, and the surrounding Dorset countryside.

In Conclusion: Camping Near Tyneham is a Great Option

While you can’t pitch a tent in Tyneham village itself, the campsites nearby let you experience the area’s natural beauty and history without compromise. Whether you prefer a quiet rural spot or a coastal site with dramatic views, there’s something for everyone.

Staying close by means you can easily visit Tyneham on open days, walk the range paths to Worbarrow Bay, and explore the Jurassic Coast at your leisure – all while enjoying the freedom of camping in one of England’s most beautiful corners.
